Chappan Dukan

2026 · Academic Studio

Chappan Dukan reimagines one of Indore’s most iconic public food streets through contemporary urban design and spatial experience. The project focuses on enhancing pedestrian interaction, visual identity, public gathering spaces, and atmospheric street activation while preserving the cultural vibrancy of the original urban fabric.
